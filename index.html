Game Rules

OBJECTIVE

The objective of Beat That Dice Game is to win maximum rounds. The game is played with two six-sided dice.

GAMEPLAY

Each player rolls dice on his turn and scores the highest value number which can be made with the two dice numbers. The player with a higher score in each rounds wins the round. In the event of a tie, both players are considered winners for that round. The player winning the maximum number of rounds out of three rounds wins the game.
